Reduce Your Carbon Footprint and Inform

Stopping global warming starts with individuals making the choice to reduce their carbon footprint, and informing others to do the same, here are some ways you can reduce your impact on the environment:

  1. Speak up: inform others on their effect on climate change.
  2. Transportation: Prefer transport such as mass transit and ride sharing over personal transport.
  3. Electricity: Reduce electricity intake by purchasing more energy effecient appliances, and unplunging them when not in use.
  4. Renewable Energy: Change to more renewable forms of energy, prefer electricity over petrol/gas, such as solar power, reduce hot water usage.
  5. Effeciency: Weatherize housing to better insulate against changes in temperature, reducing heating/cooling costs
